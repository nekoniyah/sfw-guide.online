Universal SFW Guidelines for Anime and Furry Communities

Rights

  • Freedom to create and share original character designs
  • Freedom to explore artistic styles and techniques
  • Freedom to incorporate creative elements and character variations
  • Right to participate in community discussions and events

Duties

  • Must provide appropriate and full coverage clothing for all characters
  • Must ensure clothing covers any potentially suggestive or sexualized parts
  • Must maintain appropriate behavior in community interactions
  • Must report violations of community guidelines

Prohibitions

  • No anatomical exaggeration of any kind
  • No form-fitting or revealing clothing
  • No sexualized poses or expressions
  • No suggestive facial expressions
  • No implied suggestive content
  • No bypassing guidelines through creative workarounds

Enforcement

    Enforcement Violations of these guidelines will result in content removal and may lead to community access restrictions. These guidelines serve as a universal standard for maintaining appropriate content in both anime and furry communities. Other community guidelines may reference this document as a baseline for SFW content standards.
